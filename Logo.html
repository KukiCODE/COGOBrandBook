Brand book/Style Guide

Master Logo

The logo consists of a cut of V above a cut off O. They're joined together by the short end of the V which is the right arm of the V. The O is missing the upper left quarter of itself and together they form a single linear color gradient. The inside edges of the V are curved but none of the corners of the O are.

Clearspace Rules

A minimum clearspace rule has been created to make sure the logo always has clear visibility in application and ensures the logo never gets cropped off in print. The following clearspace rules are a minimum guide that should always be followed.

The Clearspace margins are equal to 50% of the logos width.

Do's

Minimum logo width:
80px (screen)
25mm (print)

The linear color gradient must
adhere to the angle of the V arm.

Don'ts

Don’t squash or stretch the logo.
Always use the logo in its
correct supplied proportions.

Don’t use the logo without all it's parts.
Apart from specific, agreed
applications, the full version of the logo
should be used at all times

Don’t change the colour or texture.
The logo should always appear
watermelon and peachy-pink, never add a colour,
texture or effect to the logo.

Don't change the angle of the gradient
The angle of the gradient should always
match the angle of the left arm of the V.
